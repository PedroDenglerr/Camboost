Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is TalkPal?
TalkPal is a language learning platform that uses the latest GPT technology to help students excel in mastering the English language through innovative modes and personalized features.

You can sign up for TalkPal by visiting our website and clicking on the "Sign Up" button. Follow the instructions to create your account.
What types of English exams can TalkPal help me prepare for?
TalkPal can help you prepare for various Cambridge English Exams such as KET, PET, FCE, CAE, and CPE.
Is there a mobile app for TalkPal?
Yes, TalkPal is available as a mobile app on both iOS and Android platforms. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play.

Currently, TalkPal focuses on English language learning. We plan to expand our offerings to include other languages in the future.
